Chicken Casserole Recipe

For those who love chicken this dish is an absolute must! This dish has all my favorite foods mixed in one. The biscuit crust is fluffy and the cheese and vegetables make the chicken to die for.

Ingredients

Here is a list of the many food items required to make this masterpiece of Culinary Architecture

  • Three to four Chicken Filets (8-10 strips)
  • Four cups Premier Biscuit Mix
  • Nine to twelve thin slices of Velveeta Cheese
  • One cup sliced Carrot
  • Two cups diced Potatoes
  • One cup of cut Broccoli
  • (Optional) One cup of Peas
  • Two cups of Milk
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon Chicken seasoning
  • 2 tablespoon of Butter
