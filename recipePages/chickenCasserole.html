Chicken Casserole Recipe

For those who love chicken this dish is an absolute must! This dish has all my favorite foods mixed in one. The biscuit crust is fluffy and the cheese and vegetables make the chicken to die for.

Ingredients

Here is a list of the many food items required to make this masterpiece of Culinary Architecture

  • Three to four Chicken Filets (8-10 strips)
  • Four cups Premier Biscuit Mix
  • Nine to twelve thin slices of Velveeta Cheese
  • One cup sliced Carrot
  • Two cups diced Potatoes
  • One cup of cut Broccoli
  • (Optional) One cup of Peas
  • Two cups of Milk
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon Chicken seasoning
  • 2 tablespoon of Butter

Intructions

Step by Step instructions on how to cook chicken casserole are as follows:

  1. Dice chicken into small pieces.
  2. Boil chicken on medium-high for 20 minutes until 165 degrees internal temperature is achieved.
  3. Boil vegetables for 10 minutes or until tender.
  4. While boiling, add biscuit mix with milk and water to a mixing bowl, mix thoroughly.
  5. (Optional) Step 10 Can be started here if you want to make a layer of biscuit on the bottom as well as on top.
  6. When done add chicken to a 2 inch deep baking pan.
  7. Add vegetables to the chicken in baking pan and mix them around the pan evenly.
  8. Add seasonings.
  9. Place the velveeta slices evenly on top of the chicken vegetable mix in a grid like pattern
  10. Cut small pieces of the biscuit dough and flatten into a square or rectangle.
  11. Place biscuit dough square on top of the chicken and vegetable and cheese.
  12. Continue adding dough squares on top of the mix until the pan is completely covered by dough.
  13. Bake for 25 minutes or until biscuit dough is golden brown and thoroughly done.
  14. Remove from the oven and dig in!
