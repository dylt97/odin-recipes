Homemade Steak Dinner Recipe

Whats more American than a juicy steak?! I'm going to tell you everything you need in order to make this down-home favorite of mine and many others.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Ribeye Steak for each person (or any cut of beef you prefer)
  • 1/2 teaspoon Montreal Steak Seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon Mesquite Seasoning
  • 2 Potatoes per persons
  • 1 pack of brown Mushroom Gravy Mix
  • 1 tablespoon of Butter
  • 2 cups of Premier Biscuit Mix
  • 1/2 cup of Milk
  • 1/3 cup of Water
  • 2 tablespoon of Vegetable oil

With these ingredients you will have everything you need in order to make the best steak dinner you have ever eaten. The instructions will contain three parts: the Steak, the potatoes, and also the biscuits.

Instructions

Steak

  1. Add 2 tablespoon of vegetable oil to a skillet (preferably cast-iron) on medium.
  2. Once hot add the steak(s) to the skillet and sprinkle seasonings on the meat.
  3. Cook for 5 minutes and flip, repeating as necessary until thouroughly cooked as desired.

Potatoes

  1. Peel and slice potatoes in thin slices.
  2. Boil potato slices in kettle on medium-high for 25 minutes or until soft.
  3. Drain potatoes and mash thoroughly with potato masher.
  4. Add 1 tablespoon butter (salt and pepper optional) and mix.
  5. For gravy mix add 1 cup water, mix, and boil for 10 minutes.

Biscuits

  1. Add biscuit mix, water, and milk to mixing bowl and mix thoroughly.
  2. Seperate biscuit dough into 2 inch round balls and flatten to one inch.
  3. Grease baking pan or spray non-stick oil onto pan and place dough on pan to bake.
  4. Bake biscuits on 350 for 15 minutes or until golden brown on top.
  5. Butter biscuits and enjoy!
